La Truite d'Argent: Vakantie accommodaties met restaurant

relaxed, welcoming hideaways in some of the most beautiful corners of the English countryside.

As the days grow longer and the gardens burst into life, our chefs and gardeners work side by side to bring you our fresh and seasonal 25 mile menu, full of vibrant spring flavours. What we can't grow ourselves in the Kitchen Garden, we source from our trusted local suppliers - the best farmers, fishermen and foragers around.

The places we're lucky enough to call home are full of life in spring - buzzing with events, blossoming walks and friendly communities that shine this time of year. And when the day's adventures are done, our cosy bedrooms are just the spot to unwind.
